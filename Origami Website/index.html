About Usㅤ

Origami is derived from two Japanese words: Ori (folded) and Kami (paper). Before that, the art was called “orikata” (folded shapes).

  1. There are more than a dozen National Origami Associations throughout the world.
  2. Akira Yoshizawa is considered the Grand Master of modern Origami. He created over 50,000 origami models, invented wet-folding, and developed a method of diagramming origami instructions.
  3. The Guinness Book of World Records has dozens of entries for origami from most folds, to smallest and biggest and more!
